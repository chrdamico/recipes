# Lemon Edamame Pasta

**Serves:** 2 (full meal)
**Tags:** vegetarian, easy, fast

*Adapted from [The Healthy Home Cook](https://www.thehealthyhomecook.com/light-fresh-lemon-edamame-pasta-5/)*

## Ingredients

### Pasta
- 200g pasta (any shape)
- 200g frozen edamame (Edamame)
- 3-4 spring onions (Fr√ºhlingszwiebeln), sliced

### Lemon dressing
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & black pepper
- Chili flakes (Chiliflocken) to taste

### Optional additions
- Parmesan shavings
- Toasted pine nuts or sesame seeds
- Fresh basil or mint

---

## Instructions

### 1. Cook pasta and edamame together
Boil pasta in salted water. When almost done, add frozen edamame to the pot and simmer until both are tender (2-3 minutes more). Drain.

### 2. Make the dressing
Whisk together lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, and chili flakes.

### 3. Combine
Toss hot pasta and edamame with the dressing and sliced spring onions.

### 4. Serve
Top with parmesan, toasted seeds, or fresh herbs if you have them.

---

## Notes

- For more protein: add a fried egg on top
- For more substance: toss in halved cherry tomatoes or spinach
