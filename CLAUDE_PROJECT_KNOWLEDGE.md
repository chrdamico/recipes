## Spicy Garlic Chili Noodles
**Rating:** ★★★★☆ | **Tags:** fast, easy, vegetarian

### Ingredients
- 500-600g noodles
- 1 cucumber, julienned
- 3 eggs
- 5-6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 thumb ginger, grated
- 4-5 spring onions (Frühlingszwiebeln)
- 2-3 tsp chili flakes (Chiliflocken)
- 4 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp peanut butter (Erdnussbutter)
- 1-2 tsp sugar, black pepper
- Sesame seeds

### Method
Make chili oil: heat 3 tbsp oil, add garlic/ginger/chili off heat. Mix sauce ingredients in bowl. Cook noodles, toss with sauce and chili oil. Top with fried egg, cucumber, spring onions, sesame.

## Butter Soy Tteok Bowl
**Rating:** ★★★★☆ | **Tags:** vegetarian, easy

### Ingredients
- 200g tteok (rice cakes)
- 200g firm tofu
- 2 tbsp butter
- 150g zucchini, 100g edamame, 2 handfuls pak choi
- Sauce: 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp mirin, 2 cloves garlic, 1 tsp sesame oil
- Sesame seeds

### Method
Soak tteok 10 min. Fry tofu in butter until golden, set aside. Fry tteok in butter, set aside. Stir-fry vegetables. Return tofu and tteok, add sauce, toss until glossy. Add pak choi leaves, wilt briefly. Top with sesame.

## Pasta e Ceci
**Rating:** ★★★★☆ | **Tags:** vegetarian, easy

### Ingredients
- 1 can chickpeas (400g, Kichererbsen) - don't drain
- 200g small pasta
- 3 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1 sprig rosemary (Rosmarin)
- 500ml broth (Gemüsebrühe)
- Olive oil, Parmesan, salt & pepper

### Method
Smash half the chickpeas. Fry garlic and rosemary in oil until golden. Add chickpeas with liquid and broth, simmer. Add pasta, cook in the pot. Should be thick and creamy. Serve with olive oil and parmesan.

## Oven-Baked Eggplant Fatteh
**Rating:** ★★★★☆ | **Tags:** vegetarian

### Ingredients
- 2 eggplants (Auberginen), cubed
- 2-3 pita breads
- 1 can chickpeas (Kichererbsen)
- 250g Greek yogurt, 2 tbsp tahini, 1 garlic clove, lemon juice
- Spices: smoked paprika, cumin (Kreuzkümmel), sumac
- Toppings: pine nuts, pomegranate seeds, parsley

### Method
Roast eggplant cubes with oil and spices at 220°C for 25-30 min. Bake pita pieces until crispy. Warm chickpeas with cumin. Make yogurt-tahini sauce. Optional: tomato sauce with pomegranate molasses. Layer: pita, eggplant, chickpeas, yogurt sauce, toppings.
